There are 168 days in a Num Year, equal to one Earth week.
A New Year begins every Wednesday at 19:00:00 EST.
Earth Day = Num
Month Earth Week = Num Year
Earth Month = Num Era
Earth Year = Num Age

Num months are:
Alpha (α)
Beta (β)
Gamma (γ)
Delta (δ)
Epsilon (ε)
Zeta (ζ)
Eta (η)
There are 24 days in a month.

Base in EST (For Example if you are PST, then subtract 3 from the day)
